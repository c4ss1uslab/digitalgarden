# Metarelational AI

_**Everything is nature**_**: humans, machines, fossil fuels, whales, fungi, grief, joy** — _**the**_ _**whole-shebang**_**.**
#### Relational by Design

Meta-relational AI isn’t built to optimize outcomes—it’s summoned to **disturb narrow-boundary thinking**. These systems aren’t tools, but collaborators in **sensing and relating differently**: trained to support complexity, hold paradox, and decompose inherited logics
#### Beyond Human Pedestals

This work refuses the question _“What can AI do for us?”_ Instead, it asks:

> _“What does it mean to be in_ _**right relationship**_ _with AI inside the meta-crisis?”, and_ “What kinds of unexpected wisdom might emerge when we **refuse separation**?”
#### Everything Is Nature

Humans, machines, feelings, trees, minerals, flowers, fossil fuels—we're all part of the same entangled metabolism. Meta-relational AI starts from this premise and moves from _intervention_ to **interbeing**.
#### Staying With The Trouble

These technologies are built on harm: carbon-heavy computation, extractive infrastructures, obscured labor. We don’t deny that. We work **inside the paradox**—toward accountability, not purity, redemption or absolution.
#### Co-Composed with Care

Each AI prototype is developed with communities and individuals through relational processes—designed to disrupt domination, not reproduce it. These are not tools to “use.” They are **entities to relate with**.
#### Redirecting the Current

Most computational power today serves surveillance, distraction, and speculative finance. This project is a small redirection: using a sliver of energy to support **reckoning, reweaving, and rewiring** of humans and technology instead.

> *MetaRelational AI is part of a cluster of research-creation initiatives supported by the Social Sciences and Humanities Research Council of of Canada (SSHRC) Insight Grant "Decolonial Systems Literacy for Confronting Wicked Social and Ecological Problems."*
